Australia 2003

November 2003 saw me finally leave Britain (all be it for one month), as I went out to join two friends from work - John and Kris - during their adventures 'down under' I spent the majority of my time in Sydney - staying at the Glebe Village Backpackers hostel on the outskirts of the city. I also traveled north to Cairns for a few days towards the end of my trip so that I could check out the Great Barrier Reef and Rainforest - and as it turned out see some sunshine too!
